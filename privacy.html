5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored in:

Cloud Storage (Firebase/Google Cloud):

  • Primary data storage in Google Cloud Platform
  • Servers located in the United States
  • Replicated across multiple regions for reliability
  • Subject to Google's security and privacy practices

Local Device Storage (SwiftData):

  • Cached data for offline functionality
  • Local profile and settings
  • Encrypted using iOS device encryption
  • Stored only on your device

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards:

  • End-to-end HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Firebase Security Rules to control data access
  • Authentication requirements for all data access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Automated threat detection
  • Secure key management

Administrative Safeguards:

  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Background checks for employees with data access
  • Regular security training
  • Incident response procedures
  • Data access logging and monitoring

Physical Safeguards:

  • Secure data center facilities (Google Cloud)
  • Physical access controls
  • Environmental controls
  • Disaster recovery procedures

5.3 Limitations

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your phone and account secure
  • Not sharing your verification codes
  • Reporting suspected unauthorized access
  • Using strong device passcodes
  • Keeping your app updated

5.4 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach that may affect your information, we will:

  • Investigate the incident promptly
  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required
  • Take steps to prevent future incidents
  • Offer support and guidance to affected users

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Account Data

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. This includes:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Event history: Until you delete events or account
  • Messages: Until deleted or account termination
  • Groups: Until groups are deleted or you leave them
  • Usage data: Typically 2 years for analytics

6.2 Deleted Account Data

When you delete your account:

Immediate Deletion:

  • Authentication credentials
  • Profile information
  • Personal settings

30-Day Retention:

  • Event participation history (for others' event records)
  • Group memberships (to maintain group integrity)
  • Messages (visible to other participants)

Extended Retention (Legal/Security):

  • Fraud prevention records: Up to 7 years
  • Legal compliance data: As required by law
  • Security logs: Up to 1 year
  • Backup data: Up to 90 days

6.3 Backup Data

Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 90 days. Backups are:

  • Not accessible for regular operations
  • Securely encrypted
  • Automatically deleted after retention period
  • Used only for disaster recovery

Rally is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8.2 Age Verification

While we require users to be at least 13, we do not verify ages during registration. Parents or guardians who believe their child under 13 has created an account should contact us immediately.

8.3 Parental Rights

If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13:

  • We will delete the account immediately
  • We will delete all associated data
  • We will not use or share the information
  • We will notify the user's device/email if possible

Parents can:

  • Request information about data collected
  • Request deletion of their child's data

Users aged 13-17 should obtain parental consent before using Rally. We recommend parents:
